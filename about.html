ICPS-Suggestion Box

The Student Suggestion Box System is designed to improve communication between students and the school administration. It allows students to submit ideas, feedback, and concerns easily and securely.

This system encourages transparency, accountability, and active participation in school development. Students can anonymously or openly share their suggestions, and administrators can efficiently manage and respond to them.

Our goal is to foster a collaborative environment where everyone's voice is heard and valued, helping to create a better learning experience for all.
